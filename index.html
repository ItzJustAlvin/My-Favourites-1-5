My Favourite Hobby

✨ Digital Art ✨

I like doing digital art because it fulfills all of what I want. I can undo whatever and still make art. Although it might not be as realistic it's still a start to my art journey! I usually do digital art and then add it to a video or montage and I feel like it's a good use of time. You should try it our for yourself! An app which is perfect and fullfils are blanks is "Ibis Paint X". It has tons of different feautures and is the app i always use!!



There are many types of Digital Art forms!
Here are some of them I have used:




Character Tracing --

In this, you use a template of a character and trace it. Later you can fill it in to practice your shading, colouring or even drawing of that character.



Gacha Art --

In this, you use an app called Gacha Life or Gacha Club to make a character. Late you can use this character as a template and make some art with shading, textures etc..



Anime Art

In this art, you use any sort of inspiration and make it a kind of, half cartoon ~ half animation character. This is anime art..

My favourite character, Kamisato Ayaka

Daughter of the Yashiro Commission's Kamisato Clan. Dignified and elegant, as well as wise and strong.
Though Miss Ayaka is the eldest daughter of the Kamisato Clan, she is very attentive to everyone's needs. She will personally take care of the most troublesome matters. If I hadn't seen it with my own eyes, I wouldn't believe that there are noble ladies like her!
Working in tandem with her elder brother and the current head of the clan, Kamisato Ayato, the siblings divided among themselves different responsibilities arising from their noble lineage. Ayato is in charge of government affairs while Ayaka watches over the clan's internal and external affairs.
